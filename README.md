# Aerox_NextGen_

## **✈️ Business Scenario:**

Aerox Aviation is a rapidly expanding airline operating across domestic and international routes, managing thousands of passengers, flight schedules, aircraft, airport operations, crew assignments, financial transactions, and customer services.

As the airline continues to scale its operations, multiple departments rely on disconnected systems and manual processes, resulting in duplicate records, delayed reporting, inefficient resource utilization, and limited operational visibility. These operational challenges affect flight punctuality, customer satisfaction, revenue growth, and strategic decision-making.

Aerox Aviation requires a centralized, secure, scalable, and normalized Relational Database Management System (RDBMS) that serves as a single source of truth for all airline operations while enabling SQL-driven analytics, operational efficiency, and data-driven business intelligence.

---

## **🚨 The "Pain Points":**

The existing airline operations face the following challenges:

### **1. Fragmented Operational Data**

- Passenger, booking, flight, crew, maintenance, and financial records are stored across disconnected systems.
- Duplicate records and inconsistent information reduce data reliability.

### **2. Limited Operational Visibility**

- Lack of real-time visibility into flight schedules, aircraft availability, crew assignments, and operational KPIs.
- Decision-makers cannot monitor business performance efficiently.

### **3. Flight Scheduling & Resource Inefficiencies**

- Manual scheduling of flights, aircraft, and crew increases operational delays.
- Poor resource utilization affects overall airline performance.

### **4. Revenue & Financial Tracking Challenges**

- Difficult to analyze revenue by routes, booking classes, payment methods, and customer segments.
- Limited visibility into profitable and underperforming routes.

### **5. Aircraft Maintenance Challenges**

- Maintenance schedules are not integrated with operational data.
- Increased aircraft downtime causes flight delays and higher operational costs.

### **6. Slow Business Reporting**

- Reports require manual data consolidation from multiple systems.
- Delayed reporting slows operational and strategic decision-making.

### **7. Customer Experience Issues**

- Booking inconsistencies and delayed operational updates impact passenger satisfaction.
- Limited customer feedback analysis restricts service improvement.

### **8. Data Integrity & Scalability Issues**

- Lack of a normalized database causes data redundancy and inconsistencies.
- Existing systems are not scalable to support future airline growth.

---

## **🎯 The "Database Solution Mission":**

Design an enterprise-grade Airline Database Management System that:

### **1. Centralizes Airline Operations**

- Integrate passengers, bookings, flights, airports, aircraft, crew, services, revenue, and transactions into one database.
- Eliminate duplicate records and improve data consistency.

### **2. Improves Operational Efficiency**

- Streamline flight scheduling and crew allocation.
- Optimize aircraft utilization and airport resource management.
- Reduce operational delays through centralized data management.

### **3. Strengthens Revenue Management**

- Monitor revenue across routes, booking classes, and payment methods.
- Identify profitable routes and revenue opportunities.
- Support SQL-driven financial reporting.

### **4. Enhances Customer Experience**

- Improve booking accuracy and passenger information management.
- Track customer feedback and service quality.
- Increase customer satisfaction through efficient operations.

### **5. Supports Data-Driven Decision Making**

- Generate SQL-based reports and operational dashboards.
- Monitor KPIs such as revenue, bookings, aircraft utilization, and customer ratings.
- Deliver actionable business insights for management.

### **6. Ensures Data Integrity & Scalability**

- Implement a fully normalized relational database.
- Maintain referential integrity using Primary and Foreign Keys.
- Support future business expansion with a scalable database architecture.

### **7. Enables Business Intelligence**

- Analyze passenger trends, route performance, aircraft utilization, and revenue growth.
- Support real-time business insights and strategic planning.
- Build the foundation for future BI dashboards and predictive analytics.

- Improved Data Integrity & Accuracy
- Faster SQL-Based Reporting
- Better Flight Scheduling & Resource Utilization
- Enhanced Revenue Monitoring
- Improved Customer Satisfaction
- Reduced Operational Delays
- Scalable Database Architecture
